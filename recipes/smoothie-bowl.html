Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Description

Blue Smoothie bowl with assorted fruits

Ingredients

  • Frozen fruit of choice
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Ice
  • Protein Powder

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of ice
  2. Add ~2 cups of frozen fruit
  3. Add 1/4 cup of milk
  4. Add 2 spoonfuls of Greek Yogurt
  5. Add 1 cup of Protein Powder
  6. Blend everything together, then eat!
